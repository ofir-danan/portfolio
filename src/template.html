Ticket-Manager

This app uses mock data about tickets for a certain platform, and handel them by search, labels, and hiding unwanted results. You can filter the results by clicking on a libel inside a ticket, or a global labels that are changing according to the filtered results, and the existing labels in the database. By search in the search input the results filtered by every letter that's added. A ticket with a green shield next to him is a "Done" ticket, otherwise it's "UnDone". To hide a ticket, press the "Hide" button on the wanted ticket. To restore all hidden tickets hit the restore button on the top. This site use React.js, Node.js, MongoDb Atlas, Heroku
